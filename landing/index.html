Why we built this

DocuSign, PandaDoc, Adobe Sign, and friends charge $20-50 every single month - even if you only need to sign a contract twice a year.

Most people don't sign documents every day. You sign a SAFE for an investor, a lease, a contractor agreement, a school form. Maybe ten times a year if you're busy.

Yet the eSignature industry is built on monthly subscriptions. You either pay $30/month all year for occasional use, or you stop your subscription and have to re-subscribe every time something needs signing.

FreeSign exists because signing a document shouldn't require a recurring subscription. The technology is mature. The legal frameworks (ESIGN Act, UETA, eIDAS) are clear. The hosting cost per signature is fractions of a cent.

So we built a free, open-source alternative. No subscription. No upsells. No "premium" tier hiding basic features. Sign as many or as few documents as you want. The source code is on GitHub - host it yourself, or use this hosted instance.
